Real Estate Appraisal Services in Coronado, San Diego County, California

Looking for a real estate appraiser in Coronado? CA-Appraiser.com prepares certified, non-lender appraisals across San Diego County, led by Brian Ward. A resort island city connected by bridge and a military presence, Coronado is a tightly held, high-value market with limited turnover.
